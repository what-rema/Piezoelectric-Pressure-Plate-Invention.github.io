Piezoelectric Pressure Plate DIY Invention

---caption---
  1. Step 1:

    Acquire Materials

    • Piezoelectric Disc 30pcs

    • Super Capcitors (5.5v 4F) 4pcs

    • Metal Plate (12" x 12" x 1/16") etc. Alum., Stainless Steel

    • Relay Module

      HiLetgo 2pcs 5V One Channel Relay Module Relay Switch with OPTO Isolation High Low Level Trigger

    • LM358 Dual Operational Amplifier

      LM358P LM358 Dual Operational Amplifier IC Om-Amp DIP-8

    • Wiring & Electrical Tape & Glue & Diodes

  2. Step 2:

    Devise Pressure Plate

    1. Acquire Glue, Metal Plate, and Piezoeletric Discs.

    2. Take the Metal Plate and place it on a flat surface. Then take a piezo disc and put the glue on the crystal. Then place the glue side of the piezo disc on to the Metal Plate. Continue this in a grid until it looks like this:

      • Make sure everything is even & leveled

      • Make sure everthing is thin

  3. Step 3:

    Wiring Super Capcitors

    Caution: It is dangerous to have these devices touching each other without connecting it to outlet such as an L.E.D.

    Caution: this is not a toy: this is eletrical equipment so it must be handled, using normal eletronic saftey precautions.

    1. Attain Super Capcitors, Wires, and Diodes.

    2. Take half of the your pack (4/8, 2/4, 3/6 etc.)

    3. Connect the Super Capcitors in parallel.

    4. Take the negative and positive wires of the last Super Capcitor from the connected pack and connect two differnt wires to each terminal.

    5. Connect the posistive wire to a diode with the line on the diode facing inward.

    6. Now leave this with the to terminals not touching.

    7. Repeat this, but instead connect the Super Capcitors in series.

    You may add an LED to both.
